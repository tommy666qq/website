Historical Timelines

UFO Timeline

  • 1947: Kenneth Arnold sighting; Roswell Incident; Project Sign established.
  • 1952: Washington D.C. UFO Flap; Project Blue Book begins.
  • 1966-67: National UFO wave; Congressional hearings; Condon Committee study.
  • 1975: Nuclear base incursions.
  • 1989-90: Belgian UFO wave.
  • 1997: Phoenix Lights.
  • 2004: USS Nimitz Encounter.
  • 2007-12: AATIP Program.
  • 2020: Pentagon video release.
  • 2021-23: UAP Report to Congress; Congressional hearings resume; whistleblower testimony.

Abduction Timeline

  • 1957: Antonio Villas Boas case (Brazil).
  • 1961: Betty and Barney Hill abduction.
  • 1966: "The Interrupted Journey" book published.
  • 1975: Travis Walton abduction.
  • 1980s: Systematic abduction research begins.
  • 1985: Whitley Strieber experiences.
  • 1992: MIT abduction study.
  • 1994: John Mack's "Abduction" published.
  • 1995: False memory syndrome debate.
  • 2000s: Decline in reports.
  • 2020-Present: Modern investigations focus on neuroscience and consciousness studies.

Mutilation Timeline

  • 1967: "Lady" the horse - first modern case.
  • 1970-73: Initial cattle cases in Colorado, Nebraska, Kansas.
  • 1975-76: Great cattle mutilation wave.
  • 1974-78: FBI investigation.
  • 1979-80: Rommel investigation (Operation Animal Mutilation).
  • 1980s: UFO connection develops.
  • 1989: "A Strange Harvest" documentary.
  • 1993: Fyffe, Alabama outbreak.
  • 1990s-2000s: International cases.
  • 2009-12: Colorado Springs outbreak.
  • 2019-Present: Modern cases and DNA analysis.

Disclosure Timeline

  • 1969: Project Blue Book ends; government UFO secrecy period begins.
  • 1974-78: FOIA releases begin.
  • 1980: Cash-Landrum incident.
  • 1995: Roswell military explanation (Project Mogul).
  • 2005-09: International government releases.
  • 2017: NYT reveals Pentagon UFO program (AATIP).
  • 2020: Pentagon video release.
  • 2021: UAP Report to Congress.
  • 2022-23: Congressional hearings resume; whistleblower testimony.
  • 2024-Present: Ongoing disclosure process, new legislation, global transparency movement.
